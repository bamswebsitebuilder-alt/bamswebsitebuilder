About the Author

Kade Mercer

Kade Mercer writes action-packed thrillers that explore secret organizations, government conspiracies, dangerous technology, and the personal cost of uncovering the truth.

His stories combine international suspense with emotionally driven characters who must survive impossible situations while deciding who they can trust.

The American Terrorist series introduces readers to a world where every mission has consequences and every secret creates a new enemy.
